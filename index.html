Frequently Asked Questions

How do I schedule a cleaning service?

You can schedule a cleaning service by calling our customer service number, using our online booking system, or sending us an email. We'll get back to you promptly to confirm your appointment.

Are your cleaning products safe for pets and children?

Yes, we use eco-friendly, non-toxic cleaning products that are safe for your family members and pets. If you have specific concerns or allergies, please let us know, and we can adjust accordingly.

How often should I schedule a cleaning service?

This depends on your specific needs. We offer weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, and one-time cleaning services. Our team can help you determine the best frequency based on your lifestyle and requirements.

Do I need to be home during the cleaning service?

No, you don't need to be home. Many of our clients provide a key or access code. Our cleaners are bonded and insured for your peace of mind. However, if you prefer to be present, that's completely fine too.
